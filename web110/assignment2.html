The first activity I completed was about the topic of Chrome Browser help center and advanced settings. This informed me more about the customization features Chrome has available, and how Chrome can assist us with various things, such as security, managing passwords, controlling privacy and data, customizing displays such as fonts, light and dark mode, wallpapers, and more. The second activity I completed was about Safari’s resources and features. This informed me all about Safari’s features to customize your home page to make browsing easier, safe, and more accessible to your favorite sites. Something I learned during this activity is Safari’s privacy report feature. The feature informs you of trackers who were blocked from tracking you visiting websites. Apple gives you the option of viewing a report, and managing your privacy by becoming more aware of sites you may visit, and prevent cross site tracking through device settings.
